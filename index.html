About Us

   
     
       

Nestled in the heart of Kadiyam, Andhra Pradesh, Suhanveer Exim carries forward a rich heritage of horticultural excellence. Kadiyam is renowned for its thriving nurseries and high-quality plants, and our company is proud to source our offerings direct from Kadiyam. With generations of growers perfecting the craft, we deliver true heritage plants rooted in tradition. Each plant we export is carefully selected from this famous nursery belt, ensuring that customers receive the freshest, healthiest specimens. At Suhanveer Exim, quality is our top priority. We specialize in a diverse range of greenery, from vibrant ornamental plants and stately landscaping trees to versatile indoor and outdoor varieties. Our collection even includes the latest air-purifying plants for healthier living spaces. Through rigorous cultivation and selection, we promise only premium-quality, fresh nursery plants that are ready to flourish in their new homes. Every sapling is nurtured with meticulous care — healthy, well-rooted, and plantation-ready.

     
     
             
   
 
 
   

Our Vision & Mission

   
     
       

“Grow Green, Breathe Clean”

       

To make Earth green again by spreading the love of living plants far and wide. We strive to fill every home, garden, and cityscape with lush greenery—one sapling at a time—to restore fresh oxygen, enrich lives, and nurture a healthier planet.

     
     
             
   
 
 
   

Why Choose Us?

   
     

We believe in sustainability and spreading greenery for all. Our farming practices are eco-friendly and responsible, using organic methods and water-wise cultivation to nurture the plants we love. By promoting affordable pricing and value, we make it easy for landscapers, nurseries, and hobbyists alike to enhance their projects and spaces with lush greenery. With Suhanveer Exim, going green doesn’t break the bank — it brings communities together in the shared goal of a greener planet. Our passion for nature drives everything we do. We see every exported tree or houseplant as a way to bring the purity of green beauty into homes, gardens, and projects. It’s more than commerce — it’s a mission to connect people with nature’s calming presence. We are committed to inspiring a deeper appreciation for plants, helping our customers experience the joy and serenity that healthy, living greenery can provide.
